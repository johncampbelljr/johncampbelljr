Lead Architect/Director of Engineering

Lead a team of eight engineers, reported to the CTO. Responsible for product coodination, development planning and development execution (scrum-master).

mSpoke was a venture capital funded startup that addressed information overload by creating personalized news filters utilizing machine-learning and information retrieval technologies. The mSpoke system would learn your reading habits and cull best articles from a set of news sources. The system parsed and categorized hundreds of thousands of news sources. The system, written mostly in java, used a distributed message-based architecture that enabled efficient processing of the news sources and learning algorithms. mSpoke's technology was acquired by LinkedIn.
